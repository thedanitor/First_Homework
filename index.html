How to Make a Sandwich

  1. Retrive two pieces of bread
  2. Spread mayo, mustard, or other condiment on te face up side of each piece
  3. Place a few slices of deli meat on one of the pieces (skip step if don't eat meat)
  4. Place several slices of cheese on top of the meat to cover the surface area
  5. Place tomato slices on top of the cheese
  6. Place avocado slices on top of the tomato slices
  7. Place spinach on top of the avocado
  8. Place the other piece of bread on top condiment side down
  9. Enjoy sandwich!
